# Limitations

This validation path has explicit constraints.

1. Public institutional datasets are mapped with proxy rules, not full underwriting logic.
2. Field definitions and availability vary across dataset versions and release years.
3. HMDA is application/action-focused and does not provide complete performance outcomes for all records.
4. Freddie/Fannie performance files may require release-specific joins and lifecycle interpretation.
5. CPD estimation output is draft-only and requires domain-expert/model-risk approval before operational use.
6. Fairness diagnostics here are descriptive and are not fairness certification.
7. No production-validation claim is made.
8. No customer data and no PII are required by this workflow.
